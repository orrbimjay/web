COMPANY HISTORY

ORRBIMJAY PHARMA was registered with the Department of Trade and Industry on August 6, 1986 and was issued a Certificate of Registration No. 86-23880. It is operating as a single proprietorship with Mr. Ronieldo David as a proprietor and General Manager. Its main office is located at #16 Mt. Rainier St., Filinvest 1, Quezon City.


ORRBIMJAY PHARMA started its operation thru the support and initiative given by Mr. Orr Chuan Lim, Mr. Aubim Heyndrickx and especially Mr. Jason Chee, where ORRBIMJAY’S name was derived from.


It was only in August 1987 that actual operations took place when the establishment imported from Singapore, packaging materials, mother company of which is in United States on a 150-day terms.


In 1988, the establishment imported foreign made vials from European countries in addition to its regular importations from Singapore. The local transactions on the buy and sale of locally made medical equipments with pharmaceutical laboratories in the Philippines, was however, pushed through.


The operation of Orrbimjay Pharma was/is with the joint efforts and initiative of the spouses, Ronieldo David and Ma. Luisa David.
